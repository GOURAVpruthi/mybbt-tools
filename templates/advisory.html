FAQs

Common Questions Answered

What GST services do you provide?
We provide complete GST services including registration, monthly and quarterly GSTR-1/3B filing, annual GSTR-9/9C filing, GST reconciliation (GSTR-2A/2B matching), notice handling, litigation support, ITC optimization, and advisory on GST compliance strategy.
Do you handle GST notices and litigation?
Yes, we have a specialized GST litigation team. We handle scrutiny notices, demand orders, cancellation threats, appeals before the Appellate Authority, and representation before GST authorities. Reach out immediately if you've received a notice.
Can startups and freelancers use your services?
Absolutely. We serve freelancers, consultants, early-stage startups, and growing businesses. We have specially designed packages for each business stage — from basic GST compliance to full-stack financial management.
Are the MYBBT tools free to use?
Several MYBBT tools like the GST Reconciliation Tool and PDF Tools are available on the platform. Advanced features with AI, bulk processing, and analytics will have subscription plans. Visit mybbt.co.in to explore all tools.
How quickly do you respond to queries?
All client queries are responded to within 4 business hours via email. For urgent matters like GST notices, we have priority response. Our email support is available 6 days a week.
How do I get started?
Simply fill the contact form below or email us at youradvisor.ca@gmail.com. We'll assess your needs, explain the process, and create a compliance plan. No commitment required for the initial consultation.
